# Hi! My name is Esteban Romero

I look to create new ways to explore the worlds within. Here you will find some of my games, web dev, and biosensor-based experiences. 

Currently, I am a graduate student at NYU's Game Center. I am also working as a researcher at MindHive. My background is in Biomedical Engineering at Tecnológico de Monterrey in Guadalajara, México.

## Portfolio

You will find my [portfolio](https://esromerog.com/) in my main repository and published at the site:
https://esromerog.com/projects

It was built by myself using web-based frameworks, including
* Next.js / React
* Blender for the 3D Models
* p5.js for the background visuals
* GSAP & motion for the animations
* GLSL for the backgrounds

Feel free to explore or dive directly into my projects by exploring the rest of my GitHub or going [directly to the projects](https://esromerog.com/projects)).

### AI Usage Policy
My usage of LLMs was limited to debugging code. No text or assets were AI-generated.
