It all begins by creating a feeling, and translating it into music. Mynd Music is a project where we seek to create music based on a person's feelings throughout a VR experience.

It was presented at INC Monterrey 2022 on behalf of Laboratorio Arte, A.C.

The core team consisted of five people tasked with music production, analysis, connectivity and the design of the physical installation.

## Role
Designed and programmed the VR experience, led the design of the data analysis pipeline, and coordinated the 3D artist and composer.

## Tools
Unity 3D · MNE Python · BrainFlow · TCP Networking

## Background
I wanted to make this project an exploration of different environments within our minds. The experience walks the subject through changing environments that are meant to reflect various emotional states, from the peace of being connected to nature, to the dread of anxiety and isolation. Meanwhile, the EEG analysis is based on emotional models of valence and arousal [[1]](https://doi.org/10.1111/1467-8721.00003) measured with consumer-grade devices [[2]](https://doi.org/10.3389/fnhum.2021.745135).
