A web platform to understand the data our bodies generate in creative ways. It connects metrics derived from physiological data to p5.js for visualization or sonification.

Built for educational purposes in collaboration with Professor Suzanne Dikker at NYU.

## Role
I led the programming of the web application, which included designing the frontend, backend, and agentic AI deployment. I also programmed most of the public visualizations on the platform. Throughout this time, I've also led and helped other teachers and partners in workshops and implementations.

## Tools
React · KeystoneJS · EMOTIV EEG · Muse EEG · LSL · MediaPipe · LangGraph AI Agents · [Face Emotion Analysis](https://justadudewhohacks.github.io/face-api.js/docs/index.html)

## Reach
The platform has been used by multiple high school classroom in Boston and NYC for data science curriculum. We collaborated with [NYSCI](https://nysci.org) for small workshops to help their explainers.

## MoBI Conference
The platform was showcased at the [5th International MoBI Conference](https://www.zrs-kp.si/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Zbornik-povzetkov-MOBI-CONFERENCE_ONLINE1.pdf#page=52).
